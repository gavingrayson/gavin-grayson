The history of basketball

Basketball was founded on june 6th 1946 in new york city. Basketball was
later invited the NBA or the national basketball association. The NBA is made
up of 30 different teams competing in different states. The NBA is based in
the United States. They also participate in the olympics as team USA. The NBA
now has an average of two million viewers and has made over 78 billion in revenue.
